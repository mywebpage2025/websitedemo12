Feeding Dogs

Dogs need a balanced diet including proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals. Feed them high-quality dog food and avoid giving them chocolate, onions, or cooked bones.

Feeding Cats

Cats are obligate carnivores and need meat-based foods. Choose cat food that lists meat as the primary ingredient. Never feed cats dog food, as it lacks vital nutrients like taurine.
